About Ibaraki

Ibaraki Prefecture consistently supplies the largest volume of high-quality produce to the Tokyo Metropolitan Central Wholesale Market, where Japan's finest ingredients converge. Benefiting from its location within a 100-kilometer radius of Tokyo and excellent transportation links, Ibaraki boasts abundant water resources and a climate blessed with distinct seasons. This has enabled it to achieve the highest volume of produce handled at the Tokyo Metropolitan Central Wholesale Market in Japan.

Ibaraki Prefecture, home to Japan's largest market, boasts an abundance of high-quality, delicious vegetables and fruits. It features unique local brand ingredients and varieties cultivated exclusively within the prefecture, many of which are recognized by top chefs at Michelin-guide-listed restaurants.
